National Symbols

The Tulip
The national flower of the Netherlands, originally imported in the 16th century.
Klederdracht
Traditional Dutch clothing, often featuring the famous pointed white hats (Kanten muts).
Windmills
Historic structures used for drainage and milling, now a symbol of Dutch engineering.
